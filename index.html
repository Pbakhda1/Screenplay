Compete while you ride. Win rewards you can tip or save.

ScreenPlay is a rectangular block installed in the back trunks of Uber/Lyft vehicles, with a passenger-facing monitor. Riders opt in, choose a small bet, get matched with another ScreenPlay-equipped vehicle on a similar route, and earn points by passing more ScreenPlay cars.

FAQ

Common questions about gameplay, fairness, and payouts.

How does matching work?

In production, the system matches rides by route similarity, time overlap, and availability of ScreenPlay-equipped vehicles. The demo uses a simulated match.

How are points counted?

Points increase as your vehicle passes other ScreenPlay vehicles. Production could use GPS proximity + device verification to prevent spoofing.

Is “betting” legal?

Any wager-like mechanic must be designed carefully around state laws, age requirements, and licensing. Many apps use “sweepstakes”/rewards structures instead of gambling.

Can rewards be used as tips?

Yes — rewards can be redeemable as tips or as discounts for current/future rides depending on partnerships.
